Who We Are

At MJS CONSTRUCTION, we offer a wide range of engineering services for a diverse series of clients. With decades of experience in-house, we offer engineering services in Colorado, Florida, and surrounding areas. Our company is headquartered in Lakewood, CO, originally launching in 2015. We provide various first-class engineering services including structural design and inspections, retaining walls, permit packages, project management, general consulting, MEP (mechanical, electrical, and plumbing), and building design services. We are proud to have consistently received high ratings for the work that we carry out, much of which is generated via word-of-mouth, with growing numbers of clients learning about us via social media. Many reviews include Angi certification.
